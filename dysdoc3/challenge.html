Challenge Instructions

Note: these instructions are current as of August 29, 2018 and are subject to revision.

The basic idea is to show off your dynamic documentation tool to a panel of judges, plus the audience. We envision the following steps:

  1. The night before we will email you the class (one from the same Apache POI project) to prepare docs for. That will be the focus of the demo.
  2. The Challenge. The team will come up on stage, answer interview questions (about background and motivation, not technical/test questions). Then, live generate docs, discuss the output, and answer questions from the judges.
  3. During lunch you will need to prove to the judges that the doc is generated live. This will be accomplished by having the judge ask your team to change (delete) certain parts of the target class, and regenerate the docs, which should be different from what was demoed. It is not acceptable to cache generated docs for all classes.
  4. Post-lunch, give a lightning talk of 3 mins (1-2 slides) about the cool ideas under the hood of your approach.
